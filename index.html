Prototypes, experiments, and past projects

  • Bookpx. Helps anyone find new books for themselves or others by analyzing Twitter activity.
  • Babl. A mobile app that enables users to see what people are saying about specified topics and to share their own topics with others.
  • Congressional Primaries. An award-winning set of automated, data-driven tools to help voters better understand their choices in Illinois’ 2012 congressional primaries.
  • TweetCast. Obama or Romney? TweetCast predicted which candidate you were likely to vote for in the 2012 U.S. presidential election by analyzing your Twitter activity.
  • LocalAngle. Local Angle is an application that helps content curators and consumers discover articles that may be of local interest even if they don’t originate locally.
